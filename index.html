Joomla Experience:

Joomla was the first CMS that I got to use within a company and have used on 30 or more websites including schools, colleges and small to medium businesses.

The advantages of using Joomla is that it is more of a off the shelf type solution with many modules and themes available that you can use. Development time is usually low compared to Drupal.
